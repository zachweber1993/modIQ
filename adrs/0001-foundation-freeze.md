# ADR-0001: Foundation Freeze

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The constitutional specifications have completed architectural review and cross-specification reconciliation.

The project is transitioning from product definition to technical specification.

## Decision

The following specifications are considered Frozen Drafts:

- Vision.md
- Principles.md
- Glossary.md
- ProductSpecification.md
- Architecture.md

Future specifications shall derive from these documents.

Changes to the frozen specifications require a demonstrated architectural defect or an approved architectural decision.

## Consequences

- Stable terminology
- Stable product direction
- Stable architectural boundaries
- Reduced documentation drift
- Technical specifications can proceed with confidence
